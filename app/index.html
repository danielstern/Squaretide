Squaretide

Can you save the polygon princess from the rhomboid renegades?

Tap two adjacent squares to switch them.

Line up squares of the same color to collect points.

Obtain bonus points for multiple chains and combos.

Reach the target score before time runs out to proceed to the next level.

Some things may change as the level increases...

  • Target score
  • Size of the playing field
  • Tiles need to make a chain
  • Amount of colors
